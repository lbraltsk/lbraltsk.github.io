Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Astronomy at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I recently graduated from Macalester College with a B.A. in mathematics and physics with an astronomy emphasis. My research interests include galaxy formation/evolution, data science, and astronomical instrumentation, particularly at radio frequencies. Please see my CV for more information on my academic background.

While I was at Mac, I was involved in high power rocketry, maintaining operations for WMCN 91.7 FM, and running public observing nights at the Macalester Observatory. I was a regular columnist for ROOKIE magazine for over four years, contributing personal essays, advice, and featured playlists. I'm also an avid supporter of Minnesota United FC.

I can occasionally be counted on to tweet @lbraltsk.
