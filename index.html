Biography

I am a third-year undergraduate student of physics, astronomy, and mathematics at Macalester College in Saint Paul, MN. My primary research interests include galaxy formation/evolution, data mining of large surveys, and astronomical instrumentation, particularly at radio and millimeter/submillimeter wavelengths.

In my spare time I run, dance, dream about getting my private pilot's license, and support Minnesota United FC. I was a regular columnist for ROOKIE magazine for over four years, contributing personal essays, advice, and featured playlists.
