Biography

I am a fourth-year undergraduate at Macalester College in St. Paul, MN. I study mathematics and physics with an astronomy emphasis. My research interests include galaxy formation/evolution, data mining of large surveys, and astronomical instrumentation, particularly at radio frequencies. Please see my CV for more information on my academic background.

When I'm not in class or working on research, you might find me running around the Twin Cities, sharing my love of the universe, building high power rockets, or supporting Minnesota United FC. I was also a regular columnist for ROOKIE magazine for over four years, contributing personal essays, advice, and featured playlists.

I can occasionally be counted on to tweet @lbraltsk.
